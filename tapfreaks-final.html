Our Story

Why We Built TapFreaks

It started at a networking event. I watched talented people hand out paper cards that got stuffed in pockets and never looked at again. A week later, those connections were gone.

I knew there had to be a better way. One tap. Everything about you. Right on their phone. No app, no friction, no forgetting.

TapFreaks was built for every student, professional, freelancer, and founder who deserves to make a lasting first impression — without spending thousands on fancy cards that get thrown away.

We're a small team based in India, obsessed with quality and customer experience. Every card is hand-inspected before shipping. Your success is our success.
